Fix Your Prints & Free Files

Don't let a failed print slow you down. Every maker encounters problems while learning. Use these quick-reference solutions to identify common printing issues and get back to creating.

Common Printing Problems

The First Layer Failure

If your filament isn't sticking to the print bed, your nozzle is probably too high or the bed isn't level.

Quick Fix

  • ✔ Re-level the print bed.
  • ✔ Clean the bed surface.
  • ✔ Slow the first layer speed.
  • ✔ Print a one-layer leveling matrix.

Stringing & Cobwebs

Thin strings between printed parts usually mean your nozzle temperature is too high or your retraction settings need adjustment.

Quick Fix

  • ✔ Lower nozzle temperature by 5°C.
  • ✔ Increase retraction distance.
  • ✔ Enable travel moves.
  • ✔ Test with a temperature tower.
